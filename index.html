MechCheck (0.1)

This is a browser-based implementation of the Mechwarrior Online mechlab, allowing you to test out the feasibility of various loadouts. Select a standard mech chassis variant and then click and drag much like the standard mechlab. Items can be removed by clicking the 'x' on their right.
