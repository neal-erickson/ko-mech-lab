Mech Check

This is a browser-based implementation of the Mechwarrior Online mechlab, allowing you to test out the feasibility of various loadouts. Select a standard mech chassis and variant and click 'Go'. Loadouts can also be saved and loaded from browser-based local storage.

To create the loadout, click and drag weapons, ammo, and items from the item panel on the right. Items can be removed by clicking the 'x' on their right. Weapons are color-coded and hardpoints are displayed above each component.
